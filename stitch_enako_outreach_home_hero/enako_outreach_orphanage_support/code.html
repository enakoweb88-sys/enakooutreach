Active Program Enako, East Africa

Building a Home Where Dreams Take Flight

favorite

Every child deserves a foundation of love, education, and security.

At Enako Outreach, we believe that an orphanage should be more than just a shelter—it should be a sanctuary. We partner with local leaders and dedicated caregivers to provide a holistic environment where every child is seen, heard, and nurtured.

Through your generous support, we are transforming lives in Enako. Our initiatives focus on building modern classrooms, providing three nutritious meals daily, and ensuring regular medical check-ups. We are not just raising children; we are empowering the bright future leaders of Africa.

Donation FAQ

Transparent answers about how your contributions empower education and sustainable development across African communities.

Where do my donations go?

expand_more

100% of public donations go directly to funding our on-the-ground education and community development initiatives. This includes school building, teacher training, and providing learning materials. Our operational and administrative costs are fully covered by a separate group of private donors and foundations, ensuring your gift reaches those who need it most.

Impact Transparency

Every dollar is tracked and reported annually.

Direct Funding

No middleman fees on your contribution.

receipt_long

Are my donations tax-deductible?

expand_more

Yes, Enako Outreach is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization. Donations made by residents of the United States are tax-deductible to the full extent allowed by law. We provide immediate digital receipts via email for all online donations, and annual summaries are sent every January for your tax filing purposes.

verified_user

Is my payment information secure?

expand_more

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with world-class payment processors like Stripe and PayPal. Your credit card information never touches our servers, and we adhere to strict PCI compliance standards. We prioritize your privacy and will never sell or share your personal information with third parties.

history_edu

How will I receive project updates?

expand_more

We believe in the power of stories. Donors receive quarterly impact reports via email, featuring photos, videos, and direct updates from the field. You'll see the schools being built and meet the students whose lives you're changing. You can also opt-in to our monthly newsletter for more frequent, bite-sized updates.

Ready to make an impact?

Your support helps provide essential resources to communities in need. Every contribution counts.
